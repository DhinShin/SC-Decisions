But if you’ve ever asked advice on the subject, or you’re simply on the fence, the real question is this.
Do I wait and see CIG complete the game before deciding, or do I become a backer right now?

Both choices are completely viable, but the reasons for picking either are personal to you, and knowing which choice works best for you is very important.
