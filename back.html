Backing has always been for the brave. CIG has had some rocky periods and people have demanded refunds. There is one fact that new backers have to appreciate, you have a plethora oof evidence that led you to this choice, but the older backers had nothing but a demo in 2012 a speech and a lot of wild promises, some of which were promptly broken.
Looking at potential pros and cons.
Pros:
You will be able to help push the direction of the game with your feedback
You will be part of a growing community
You get to experience the evolution of each game mechanics as they are introduced and influence each step
You’ll gain rare insight of the development cycle
As a backer you’ll have a knowledge head start over any new player, even with a ship disadvantage
Cons:
The wipes really do hurt
The game is currently waiting for key technologies to be implemented which means missing features and poor performance
The backer community is literally seen as a cult, it’s almost up there with claiming to be a Trump supporter after January 6th
The pressure from your subconscious to continually increase your pledge is great, you will constantly see new things you want
Server and client crashes are brutal, never start a trade knowing it’s 100% of your funds, you can lose it all real fast
Conclusion: This choice is for the adventurous, win or lose you’ll experience a journey. But remember, no matter how much you put in you won’t get any money back out. Nor should you put in any money you will regret not having later. The money you give to CIG will be gone, what you get back isn’t something you can hold, nor will it keep your stomach filled. But I hope that at the end the experience will be worth it.
