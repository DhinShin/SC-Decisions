First, the ‘wait and see’ option. I honestly see this the prudent choice, the choice of the realist. CIG have received more than $400m at this point, and they’ll receive even more soon. They don’t need your money to finish either game, and until they are released, both titles are promises with no defined timescales and no real surety of completion.
Looking at potential pros and cons.
Pros:
If the game is good, there is no risk of buying into a dead product
You will get to buy after seeing final reviews
You will get a refined and optimised product
You will join after the soul-destroying data wipes
You will have no pressure to buy new ships as they are released, just earn it in game
Cons:
You won’t get to contribute anything to the final game
You can never be part of the testing community
You will only explore the game in its finished state
You will have no Alpha stories from before features were implemented
You will feel no excitement from the current three-month patch cycle CIG are working with
Conclusion: It could be scepticism, frugality, or maybe even total conviction that this is a scam. But it almost goes without saying, that the you of today, is not a Citizen of today. Maybe you’ll back at a later point, maybe you’ll wait until the finished game, or maybe Star Citizen will never reach a point where you consider it worth your time. But in any case, I hope you find a game you love playing soon. Star Citizen isn’t the only game worth talking about, but it’s the only one on this page.
